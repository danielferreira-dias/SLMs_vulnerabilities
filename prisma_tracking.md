## Search Strategy

### Inclusion Criteria
- [ ] Published between [START_YEAR] and [END_YEAR]
- [ ] Focus on Small Language Models (SLMs) or models < 10B parameters
- [ ] Addresses security vulnerabilities, attacks, or adversarial aspects
- [ ] Peer-reviewed or high-quality preprint
- [ ] English language

### Exclusion Criteria
- [ ] Focuses exclusively on large models (>10B parameters)
- [ ] No security/vulnerability component
- [ ] Non-English
- [ ] Duplicate publication
- [ ] Unable to access full text

### Search Terms (to be refined)
